Healthcare Capacity in the COVID-19 Pandemic in the USA

COVID-19 cases are increasing daily in the USA and there is a concern that the number of cases could overwhelm the healthcare system, as it has in other countries. We aim to help visualize healthcare capacity (ICU and hospital beds) in conjunction with COVID-19 cases and deaths. Use this website to see, in real-time, how the United States healthcare capacity of each county compares to their need as the COVID-19 pandemic progresses. This is designed to be a solution to help public health workers, civil servants, and health care workers and administrators based in the USA to track their countyâ€™s needs, respond to the pandemic and plan for the numbers of ICU and hospital beds needed.
